Our Repair Story

Trusted tech repair services in Petion-Ville since 2023

Our Mission

At AbbyTech Repair, we're committed to providing fast, affordable, and reliable repair services for all your devices. We believe everyone deserves access to quality tech repairs without the premium price tag.

Our certified technicians combine technical expertise with genuine care for your devices, ensuring they're repaired to the highest standards.

Our History

Founded in 2023 by Abigail Laurent, AbbyTech Repair started as a small kiosk in downtown Petion-Ville with just one technician. What began as a passion for fixing electronics has grown into one of the most trusted repair shops in the area.

Today, we operate from our modern workshop on Main Street, serving hundreds of satisfied customers each month while maintaining our commitment to personalized service.
